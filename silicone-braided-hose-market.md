<p><p>The silicone braided hose market comprises primarily two types: platinum cured and peroxide cured hoses. Platinum cured hoses are manufactured using a platinum-based catalyst, resulting in higher purity, flexibility, and temperature resistance, ideal for medical and food applications. In contrast, peroxide cured hoses utilize a peroxide catalyst, offering cost-effectiveness and durability, making them suitable for industrial and automotive uses. Both types cater to diverse applications, ensuring high-performance solutions tailored to specific needs across various industries.</p></p>
