<p><p>The high-end ICU ventilator market is divided into two main types: invasive and non-invasive ventilation. Invasive ventilation involves the insertion of a tube into the patient's airway, allowing for precise control of breathing and support in severe respiratory failure scenarios. Non-invasive ventilation, on the other hand, provides respiratory support through external masks or devices, enhancing patient comfort and reducing the risk of infections. Both types are essential for critical care, catering to varying patient needs and clinical conditions.</p></p>
