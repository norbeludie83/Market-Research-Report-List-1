<p><ul><li>EPS Geofoams</li><li>XPS Geofoams</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The geofoams market consists primarily of two types: EPS (Expanded Polystyrene) geofoams and XPS (Extruded Polystyrene) geofoams. EPS geofoams are lightweight, cost-effective, and provide excellent insulation, making them ideal for applications in road construction and lightweight fill projects. In contrast, XPS geofoams offer superior moisture resistance and compressive strength, making them suitable for more demanding applications like foundations and civil engineering. Both materials contribute to sustainable construction practices, ensuring durability and efficiency in various projects.</p></p>
