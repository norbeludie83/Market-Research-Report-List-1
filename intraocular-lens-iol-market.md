<p><strong>The Intraocular Lens (IOL) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-foldable lenses</li><li>Foldable Intraocular Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The intraocular lens (IOL) market comprises two main types: non-foldable lenses and foldable lenses. Non-foldable lenses are rigid and typically made from materials like PMMA, offering durability but requiring larger incisions during cataract surgery. In contrast, foldable IOLs are designed to be inserted through smaller incisions, allowing for quicker recovery and minimal trauma to the eye. They are made from flexible materials, such as silicone or hydrophilic acrylic, enhancing patient comfort and surgical efficiency.</p></p>
