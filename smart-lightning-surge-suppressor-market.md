<p><p>The Smart Lightning Surge Suppressor Market is divided into two main types: Type 1 Surge Protectors and Type 2 Surge Protectors. Type 1 Surge Protectors are typically installed at the service entrance of a building, providing immediate protection against external surges from lightning or power grid issues. In contrast, Type 2 Surge Protectors are installed downstream within the electrical system, offering additional protection for sensitive devices by filtering out residual surges that may penetrate after the initial defense. Both types enhance electrical safety and equipment longevity.</p></p>
