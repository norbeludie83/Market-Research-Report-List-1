BE Type reactors are designed for medium pressures and are used extensively in chemical processes requiring moderate durability. CE Type reactors are engineered for lower pressure, suitable for smaller scale operations. Each type serves distinct industry needs, optimizing performance and ensuring safety in various chemical manufacturing applications.</p></p>
