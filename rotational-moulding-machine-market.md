<p><ul><li>Biaxial Rotation Type Rotational Moulding Machine</li><li>Rock and Roll Type Rotational Moulding Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotational moulding machine market features two primary types: biaxial rotation and rock and roll. Biaxial rotation machines employ simultaneous rotation around two axes, ensuring uniform thickness and consistent part quality for complex shapes. In contrast, rock and roll machines utilize a tilting motion combined with rotation, making them ideal for simpler designs and smaller production runs. Both types cater to various applications, including plastic products, offering flexibility in manufacturing processes and material use.</p></p>
