<p><p>The helicopter rotor blades market can be segmented into two main types: main rotors and tail rotors. Main rotors are critical for providing lift and controlling the helicopter's flight dynamics, often characterized by larger size and complex design. Each type plays a vital role in the helicopter's overall performance, safety, and maneuverability, influencing design, materials, and manufacturing processes.</p></p>
