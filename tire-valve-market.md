<p><p>The tire valve market is primarily categorized into rubber tire valves and metal tire valves. Rubber tire valves are lightweight, cost-effective, and commonly used in passenger vehicles due to their flexibility and ease of installation. They provide a reliable seal but may be less durable in extreme conditions. In contrast, metal tire valves are more robust and resistant to corrosion, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. They offer superior performance and longevity, appealing to commercial and off-road vehicle markets.</p></p>
