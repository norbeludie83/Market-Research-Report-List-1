<p><p>The semi-automated clinical analyzers market is categorized into three types based on channel configuration. Single-channel analyzers process one sample at a time, making them suitable for smaller labs with lower testing volumes. Dual-channel analyzers can simultaneously test two samples, enhancing efficiency for moderate throughput needs. Multi-channel analyzers, which can handle multiple samples at once, are ideal for high-capacity laboratories requiring rapid testing. This segmentation helps labs choose the right equipment based on their testing demands and operational capacity.</p></p>
