<p><p>The biological excipient market comprises two primary types: oral and topical formulations. Oral excipients are ingredients used in the development of biologic drugs administered through the gastrointestinal tract, enhancing stability and bioavailability. These may include fillers, binders, and disintegrants. On the other hand, topical excipients are used in formulations for skin applications, facilitating drug delivery through the dermal layers. They enhance absorption, stability, and user experience, supporting a variety of therapeutic treatments in both segments.</p></p>
