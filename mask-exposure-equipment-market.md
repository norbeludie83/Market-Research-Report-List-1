<p><strong>The Mask Exposure Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>E-Beam Mask Exposure</li><li>Laser Beam Mask Exposure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mask Exposure Equipment market primarily includes two types: E-Beam Mask Exposure and Laser Beam Mask Exposure. E-Beam Mask Exposure utilizes electron beams to write precise patterns onto a photoresist-coated substrate, offering high resolution and accuracy, ideal for advanced semiconductor manufacturing. In contrast, Laser Beam Mask Exposure employs lasers for patterning, providing rapid processing and flexibility, suitable for low to medium volume production. Both technologies are crucial for the development of intricate microelectronic devices.</p></p>
