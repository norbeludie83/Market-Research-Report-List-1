<p><p>The automobile dealer software market is primarily divided into two types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, offering flexibility and easy access from any device with an internet connection. This model often features subscription pricing and automatic updates. In contrast, on-premises software is installed locally on the dealer's hardware, providing greater control and customization at the cost of higher upfront investments and maintenance responsibilities. Both types cater to different dealer needs and operational preferences.</p></p>
