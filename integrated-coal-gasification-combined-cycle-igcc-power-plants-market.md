<p><strong>The Integrated Coal Gasification Combined Cycle (IGCC) Power Plants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air-blown IGCC</li><li>Oxygen-blown IGCC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Integrated Coal Gasification Combined Cycle (IGCC) power plants can be categorized into two main types: air-blown and oxygen-blown IGCC. Air-blown IGCC uses ambient air to gasify coal, resulting in lower capital costs but higher nitrogen content in the syngas, which can reduce efficiency. In contrast, oxygen-blown IGCC employs pure oxygen for gasification, yielding a cleaner syngas with fewer impurities, enhancing overall efficiency. While air-blown systems are more cost-effective, oxygen-blown systems offer superior environmental performance and energy efficiency.</p></p>
