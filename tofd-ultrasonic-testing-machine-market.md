<p><p>The TOFD (Time of Flight Diffraction) Ultrasonic Testing Machine market typically features two main types: Electronic Scanning and Sector Scanning. Electronic Scanning utilizes electronically controlled elements to rapidly direct ultrasonic waves in various directions, enabling real-time imaging of defects, enhancing inspection efficiency. Meanwhile, Sector Scanning employs mechanical movement to rotate the ultrasonic probe in a sector pattern, capturing images in a more traditional way. Both methods improve flaw detection and characterization in materials, particularly in critical applications like welding and structural integrity assessments.</p></p>
