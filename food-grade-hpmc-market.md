<p><p>The Food Grade HPMC (Hydroxypropyl Methylcellulose) market is categorized into low, middle, and high viscosity types, each serving distinct applications in the food industry. Low viscosity HPMC is used for products requiring easy dispersion and low thickness, like soups and sauces. Middle viscosity HPMC offers thicker consistency, making it suitable for bakery goods and dressings. High viscosity HPMC is ideal for applications needing optimal thickening and emulsification, such as dairy products and gelled items, enhancing texture and stability.</p></p>
