<p><p>The multi-stage thermoelectric module market is categorized into three main types: bulk thermoelectric, micro thermoelectric, and thin film thermoelectric. Bulk thermoelectric materials are typically used for large-scale applications due to their high efficiency and robustness. Micro thermoelectric modules offer compact solutions suitable for small-scale devices and wearables, providing efficient heat management in limited spaces. Thin film thermoelectric technology involves ultra-thin layers, enabling integration into various surfaces and enhancing flexibility for innovative applications in electronics and energy harvesting.</p></p>
