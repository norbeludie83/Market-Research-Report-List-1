<p><p>The dietary fiber market is primarily divided into two types: soluble and insoluble dietary fiber. Soluble dietary fiber dissolves in water, forming a gel-like substance that can help lower cholesterol and stabilize blood sugar levels, making it beneficial for heart health and diabetes management. Insoluble dietary fiber, on the other hand, does not dissolve in water and aids in digestive health by promoting regular bowel movements and preventing constipation. Both types play essential roles in a balanced diet.</p></p>
