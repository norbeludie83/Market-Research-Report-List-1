<p><ul><li>Direct Electrolytic Oxidation</li><li>Indirect Electrolytic Oxidation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electro-oxidation System market comprises two main types: Direct Electrolytic Oxidation and Indirect Electrolytic Oxidation. Direct Electrolytic Oxidation involves the application of an electric current directly to the wastewater, facilitating the oxidation of pollutants. In contrast, Indirect Electrolytic Oxidation uses electrolytes to generate reactive species that oxidize contaminants without direct current application to the wastewater. Both methods are effective for wastewater treatment, each offering distinct advantages based on the specific contaminants and treatment requirements.</p></p>
