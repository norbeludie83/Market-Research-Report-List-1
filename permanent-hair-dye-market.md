<p><p>The permanent hair dye market is primarily categorized into three types: PPDA-based hair dye, PTD-based hair dye, and other types. PPDA (para-phenylenediamine) dyes are known for their long-lasting color and vibrancy, often used in professional formulations. PTD (para-toluenediamine) dyes offer a gentler alternative with reduced allergic reactions but similar durability. Other types include natural and herbal dyes, which appeal to consumers seeking chemical-free options. Each category caters to diverse consumer preferences regarding safety, color longevity, and ingredient transparency.</p></p>
