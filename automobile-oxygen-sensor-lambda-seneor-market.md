<p><p>Automobile oxygen sensors, or lambda sensors, are critical for optimizing engine performance and emissions control. Planar sensors offer a compact design for better integration, while wide-band sensors provide precise air-fuel mixture measurements across a broader range. Titania sensors use a ceramic material for oxygen measurement, offering durability and reliability in varied conditions.</p></p>
