<p><strong>The Wireless Magnetometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Scalar Magnetometers</li><li>Vector Magnetometers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless magnetometer market includes two primary types: scalar magnetometers and vector magnetometers. Scalar magnetometers measure the total magnetic field strength in a single axis, offering simplified readings useful for applications like mineral exploration and environmental monitoring. In contrast, vector magnetometers measure magnetic field components across multiple axes, providing detailed spatial information essential for advanced applications such as geophysics, navigation, and military uses. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, enhancing magnetic field analysis and exploration capabilities.</p></p>
