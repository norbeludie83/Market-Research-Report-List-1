<p><p>The Elektromotive market is divided into two main types: on-board chargers and off-board chargers. On-board chargers are integrated into electric vehicles, converting AC power from charging stations into the DC needed to charge the vehicle's battery. They offer convenience and optimized charging while the vehicle is in use. Off-board chargers, on the other hand, are standalone units located outside the vehicle, often providing faster charging capabilities. Both types play essential roles in enhancing the efficiency and accessibility of electric vehicle charging infrastructure.</p></p>
