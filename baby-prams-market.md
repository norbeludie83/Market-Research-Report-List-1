<p><p>The baby pram market is primarily divided into two main types: travel systems and conventional prams. Travel systems integrate a stroller and an infant car seat, allowing for easy transitions from car to stroller without disturbing the baby. They offer convenience for on-the-go families. On the other hand, conventional prams are designed with a focus on comfort and durability for everyday use, featuring a traditional stroller design that often offers a smooth ride but lacks the car seat adaptability of travel systems.</p></p>
