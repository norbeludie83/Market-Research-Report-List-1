<p><p>The roofing liners market includes various types such as EPDM, TPO, PVC, and others. EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) is a durable, rubber-based material known for its weather resistance and flexibility. TPO (Thermoplastic Polyolefin) is a lightweight, energy-efficient option with strong heat-reflective properties. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) offers excellent chemical resistance and durability, making it ideal for harsh environments. Other materials include modified bitumen and asphalt, catering to specific needs. Each type serves different roofing applications, balancing cost, performance, and environmental factors.</p></p>
