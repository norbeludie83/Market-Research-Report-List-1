<p><p>The electrical steels market is primarily categorized into two types: oriented electrical steels (oriented silicon steels) and non-oriented electrical steels. Oriented electrical steels are designed for high efficiency in transformers and motors, showing superior magnetic properties in specific directions. In contrast, non-oriented electrical steels exhibit consistent magnetic performance in all directions, making them ideal for applications like electric motors and generators. These materials play a crucial role in optimizing energy efficiency in various electrical devices and systems.</p></p>
