<p><p>The Arthroscopy RF System market comprises two primary types: bipolar and monopolar wands. Bipolar wands utilize two electrodes to deliver radiofrequency energy directly to targeted tissue, minimizing thermal spread and maximizing precision, making them ideal for delicate procedures. In contrast, monopolar wands employ a single electrode, allowing for broader tissue interaction and greater energy dispersal, suitable for larger areas. Both technologies enhance surgical precision and patient recovery in arthroscopic interventions, catering to various surgical needs and preferences.</p></p>
