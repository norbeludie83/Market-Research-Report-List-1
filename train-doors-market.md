<p><p>The train doors market includes various types designed for specific functions. **External Doors** provide entry and exit points, ensuring safety and weather protection. **Internal Doors** separate compartments, enhancing passenger flow and compartmentalization. **Toilet Doors** offer privacy and accessibility, often with secure locking mechanisms. **Cabinet Doors of Operator** secure control panels and equipment, ensuring operational safety. **Cab Doors** separate the driver’s cabin from passenger areas, ensuring privacy and security. Each type is tailored to meet safety, functionality, and regulatory standards in train operations.</p></p>
