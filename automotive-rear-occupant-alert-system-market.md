<p><p>The Automotive Rear Occupant Alert System market includes two primary types: Ultrasonic Sensor Systems and Pressure Sensor Systems. Ultrasonic Sensor Systems use sound waves to detect movement or presence of rear occupants, alerting drivers if a child or pet is left behind. Pressure Sensor Systems rely on weight detection to identify occupants, triggering alerts when pressure is sensed on rear seats. Both systems enhance safety by preventing accidental neglect of passengers, especially children, in vehicles.</p></p>
