<p><p>The custom foot orthotics market comprises three main types: rigid orthosis, soft orthosis, and semi-rigid orthosis. Rigid orthosis provides maximum support and is often used for severe foot ailments, maintaining foot structure. Soft orthosis offers cushioning and shock absorption, ideal for conditions like plantar fasciitis. Semi-rigid orthosis strikes a balance, offering moderate support and flexibility, suitable for various activities. Each type caters to different foot conditions and individual needs, enhancing comfort and functionality for users.</p></p>
