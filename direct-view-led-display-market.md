<p><p>The Direct View LED Display market is segmented into three key types based on pixel pitch: less than 1 mm, 1-2.5 mm, and greater than 2.5 mm. Displays with pixel pitches less than 1 mm offer ultra-high resolution, ideal for close-viewing applications like control rooms and high-end retail. The 1-2.5 mm category balances resolution and distance, suitable for medium-sized venues. Meanwhile, displays with pixel pitches greater than 2.5 mm are typically designed for larger venues, where viewing distance is greater, prioritizing cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
