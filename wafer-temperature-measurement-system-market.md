<p><strong>The Wafer Temperature Measurement System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Non-contact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wafer temperature measurement system market is divided into two primary types: contact and non-contact. </p><p>Contact measurement systems involve physical contact with the wafer to gather temperature data, often using thermocouples or resistance temperature detectors. </p><p>In contrast, non-contact measurement systems utilize infrared sensors or thermal imaging techniques to assess temperature without touching the wafer. This method provides real-time data and minimizes contamination risk, making it suitable for sensitive semiconductor processes. Both types cater to diverse industrial applications in wafer fabrication.</p></p>
