<p><ul><li>Etching Process Lead Frame</li><li>Stamping Process Lead Frame</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lead frame materials market can be categorized into two primary types based on the manufacturing process: etching process lead frames and stamping process lead frames. Etching process lead frames involve chemical etching to create intricate designs and features, offering high precision and flexibility for complex geometries. In contrast, stamping process lead frames utilize mechanical stamping, allowing for high-volume production with lower costs. Both methods serve essential roles in the semiconductor industry, providing critical components for packaging integrated circuits.</p></p>
