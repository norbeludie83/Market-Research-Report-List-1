<p><p>The intravascular imaging device market is primarily categorized into two types: endoscopic imaging systems and intravascular imaging systems. Endoscopic imaging systems utilize minimally invasive techniques to visualize internal organs and structures, often aiding in procedures like gastrointestinal examinations. Intravascular imaging systems, on the other hand, focus specifically on visualizing the interior of blood vessels, employing technologies such as IVUS (intravascular ultrasound) and OCT (optical coherence tomography) to assist in diagnosing and guiding treatment for cardiovascular diseases.</p></p>
