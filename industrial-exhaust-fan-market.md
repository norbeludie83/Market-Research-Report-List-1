<p><p>The industrial exhaust fan market is primarily categorized into centrifugal fans, axial fans, and others. Centrifugal fans work by drawing air in and expelling it at a right angle, making them suitable for high-pressure applications. Axial fans move air along the axis of the fan, providing efficient airflow in low-pressure environments. Other types encompass mixed flow and specialized fans tailored for unique applications. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, ensuring effective ventilation and controlling air quality in various settings.</p></p>
