<p><strong>The VR Lens Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Fresnel Lens</li><li>Fresnel Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VR lens market is divided into two main types: Non-Fresnel lenses and Fresnel lenses. Non-Fresnel lenses provide high optical clarity and minimal distortion, making them ideal for premium VR experiences, although they tend to be bulkier. In contrast, Fresnel lenses are thinner and lighter, utilizing concentric grooves to bend light efficiently, making them more suitable for compact VR headsets. While Non-Fresnel lenses offer superior image quality, Fresnel lenses prioritize portability and affordability, catering to diverse user needs.</p></p>
