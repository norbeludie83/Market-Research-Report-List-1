<p><p>Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD) treatment market consists of three primary types: bronchodilators, glucocorticoids, and other therapies. Bronchodilators are essential for relaxing airway muscles, improving airflow and alleviating symptoms. Glucocorticoids, anti-inflammatory medications, help reduce airway inflammation and enhance lung function. The "other" category encompasses combination therapies, antihistamines, and novel agents that support respiratory health. Together, these treatments aim to manage symptoms, improve quality of life, and slow disease progression in individuals with COPD.</p></p>
