<p><p>The Pulse Igniter market is primarily categorized into two main types: Dry Cell and Alternating Current (AC) igniters. Dry Cell igniters utilize a battery-powered system that generates energy to create ignition pulses, making them suitable for portable applications and environments where electrical supply is limited. In contrast, Alternating Current igniters rely on an AC power source to produce ignition pulses, offering higher efficiency and consistent performance. Both types serve various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and industrial applications, enhancing ignition reliability.</p></p>
