<p><p>The Platform Screen Door (PSD) System market comprises two main types: full height and half height. Full height PSDs extend from the platform level to the ceiling, providing complete protection against accidents and environmental elements, enhancing security and comfort. Half height PSDs, typically reaching waist level, offer moderate safety while allowing for better ventilation and accessibility. Both types cater to different transit needs, with full height favored in high-density urban areas, while half height is often utilized in less crowded environments.</p></p>
