<p><p>Industrial valves play a critical role in the oil and gas market, regulating flow and pressure within pipelines and processing systems. Ball valves allow quick shut-off with minimal pressure drop, while plug valves provide reliable sealing and easy maintenance. Gate valves facilitate full flow when fully open but offer limited throttling. Globe valves are ideal for precise flow control, and butterfly valves are compact and efficient for large volume applications. Each type serves specific operational needs within the industry.</p></p>
