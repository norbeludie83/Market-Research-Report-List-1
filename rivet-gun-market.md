<p><p>The rivet gun market is categorized into three main types: manual, pneumatic, and electric rivet guns. Manual rivet guns rely on human strength to operate, making them ideal for smaller projects and less frequent use. Pneumatic rivet guns use compressed air for quick and efficient fastening, suitable for high-volume applications. Electric rivet guns offer convenience and speed, powered by electricity for automated processes, making them popular in industrial settings. Each type caters to different needs and operational environments.</p></p>
