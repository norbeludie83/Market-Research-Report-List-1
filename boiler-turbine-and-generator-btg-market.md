<p><p>The Boiler Turbine and Generator (BTG) market encompasses various technologies for power generation. Power boilers produce steam by burning fuel, which drives steam turbines. Gas turbines, using combustion gas for propulsion, offer high efficiency and rapid startup. Steam turbines convert steam energy into mechanical power, commonly paired with generators to produce electricity. Turbo generators combine turbines and generators to enhance efficiency. Heat Recovery Steam Generators (HRSG) capture and utilize exhaust heat from gas turbines, improving overall energy efficiency in combined cycle systems.</p></p>
