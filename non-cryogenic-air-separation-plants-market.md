<p><p>Non-cryogenic air separation plants utilize various technologies to separate air components without the need for low temperatures. Selective adsorption employs materials that preferentially adsorb specific gases, allowing for efficient separation. Differential permeation through membranes involves using selective membranes that allow certain gases to pass through faster than others, thus achieving separation based on permeability differences. Other methods may include pressure swing adsorption and other innovative techniques tailored to specific industrial needs, contributing to the versatility and efficiency of air separation processes.</p></p>
