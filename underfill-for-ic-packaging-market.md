<p><p>Underfill in IC packaging is critical for enhancing reliability and performance. FC (Flip Chip) Underfill involves a resin-based adhesive applied between the chip and the substrate, providing thermal and mechanical stability. BGA (Ball Grid Array) Underfill is used to fill the space beneath BGA solder balls, improving stress distribution and reducing failures. WLCSP (Wafer-Level Chip Scale Package) Underfill applies to chips where the die directly attaches to the substrate, optimizing space and performance in compact designs. Each type addresses specific packaging challenges.</p></p>
