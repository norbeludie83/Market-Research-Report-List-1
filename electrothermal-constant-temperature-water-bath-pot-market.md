<p><ul><li>Single Hole Constant Temperature Water Bath Pot</li><li>Porous Constant Temperature Water Bath</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrothermal constant temperature water bath pot market features two main types: single hole constant temperature water bath pots and porous constant temperature water bath pots. Single hole pots are designed for precise temperature control in individual samples, making them ideal for laboratory applications requiring minimal sample volume. In contrast, porous constant temperature water bath pots allow multiple samples to be heated uniformly. These pots are preferred for larger volume experiments where consistent temperature maintenance across samples is essential.</p></p>
