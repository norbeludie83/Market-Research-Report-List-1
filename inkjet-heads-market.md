<p><strong>The Inkjet Heads Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermal MEMS Inkjet Heads</li><li>Piezo MEMS Inkjet Heads</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inkjet heads market is primarily divided into two types: thermal and piezo MEMS (Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems) inkjet heads. Thermal MEMS inkjet heads utilize heat to create vapor bubbles that eject ink droplets, offering efficiency and simplicity. In contrast, piezo MEMS inkjet heads use piezoelectric materials to mechanically force ink out, allowing for more precise droplet placement and a wider range of ink types. Both technologies cater to diverse applications in commercial printing, packaging, and industrial uses.</p></p>
