<p><p>The efflorescence remover market is primarily divided into two types: acid-free and acid-based removers. Acid-free efflorescence removers are typically formulated with mild cleaning agents that effectively dissolve salt deposits without risking damage to surfaces, making them ideal for sensitive materials. In contrast, acid-based removers utilize stronger chemical formulations that can more aggressively treat stubborn efflorescence, but they may require careful handling to avoid surface degradation. Both types cater to different user needs based on the severity of efflorescence and material compatibility.</p></p>
