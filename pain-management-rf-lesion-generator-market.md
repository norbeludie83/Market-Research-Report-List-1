<p><strong>The Pain Management RF Lesion Generator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Radio Frequency (RF)</li><li>Pulsed Radio Frequency (PRF)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pain Management RF Lesion Generator market includes two primary technologies: Conventional Radio Frequency (RF) and Pulsed Radio Frequency (PRF). Conventional RF generates continuous energy to produce thermal lesions in targeted nerves, effectively interrupting pain signals. In contrast, PRF utilizes short bursts of radiofrequency energy, which create less thermal damage, maintaining nerve function while modulating pain perception. Both methodologies are essential in minimally invasive pain management, offering tailored approaches based on patient needs and specific pain conditions.</p></p>
