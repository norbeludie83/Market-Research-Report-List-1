<p><p>The ultrasonic distance meter market is primarily categorized into analog and digital types. In contrast, digital ultrasonic meters utilize electronic displays, offering precise numerical readouts and additional features such as data logging and Bluetooth connectivity. Digital types are increasingly popular due to their ease of use and enhanced functionality, while analog models appeal to users preferring straightforward, non-digital interfaces.
