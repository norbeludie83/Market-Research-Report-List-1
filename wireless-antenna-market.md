<p><strong>The Wireless Antenna Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UHF</li><li>VHF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless antenna market can be categorized mainly into UHF (Ultra High Frequency) and VHF (Very High Frequency) segments. UHF antennas operate within the 300 MHz to 3 GHz range, catering to applications like television broadcasting, mobile communications, and wireless broadband. In contrast, VHF antennas function between 30 MHz to 300 MHz, primarily used for FM radio, television signals, and maritime communications. Each segment serves distinct frequencies and requirements, influencing design, performance, and market demand.</p></p>
