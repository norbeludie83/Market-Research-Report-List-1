<p><ul><li>Cold Water Soluble PVA Grade</li><li>Hot Water Soluble PVA Grade</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Water soluble packaging films are designed to dissolve in water, catering to various applications. Cold water soluble PVA grade films dissolve in ambient temperature water, making them ideal for applications like laundry pods and single-use packaging in cooler environments. Hot water soluble PVA grade films, on the other hand, dissolve in higher temperature water, making them suitable for industrial applications and scenarios requiring rapid dissolution, such as in detergent and chemical formulations. Both types enhance convenience and sustainability in packaging.</p></p>
