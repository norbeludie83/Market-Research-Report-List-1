<p><strong>The CMOS Analog Switches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SPST</li><li>SPDT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CMOS analog switch market primarily includes Single Pole Single Throw (SPST) and Single Pole Double Throw (SPDT) switches. SPST switches control a single signal path, allowing or blocking its flow, making them ideal for simple on/off applications. In contrast, SPDT switches can route a single input to one of two outputs, enabling more complex signal management in devices. Both types are crucial in applications like audio devices, signal routing, and telecommunications, ensuring efficiency and precision in electronic systems.</p></p>
