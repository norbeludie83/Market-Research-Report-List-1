Spiral shape brushes are ideal for achieving volume and curl, as their twisted bristles capture and lift each lash. Bullet shape brushes provide a rounded tip, allowing for targeted application and easy access to lower lashes or corners. Each type serves distinct purposes, catering to various makeup styles and preferences.</p></p>
