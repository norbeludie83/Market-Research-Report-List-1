<p><p>The Hyaluronic Acid (HA) raw material market is categorized into three main types: animal tissue-sourced, synthetic, and others. Animal tissue-sourced HA is derived from biological tissues, often providing high biocompatibility and efficacy but raising ethical concerns. Synthetic HA is produced through fermentation or chemical processes, offering a more ethical and consistent alternative without the risk of animal-derived contaminants. The "others" category includes plant-derived and hybrid methods, catering to diverse applications in cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and medical devices, reflecting evolving consumer preferences.</p></p>
