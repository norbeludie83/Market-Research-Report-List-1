<p><p>Eco-friendly latex paint is a sustainable alternative to traditional paints, formulated with low or zero volatile organic compounds (VOCs), non-toxic ingredients, and environmentally friendly materials. The Asia-Pacific region is emerging as a key market due to rapid urbanization and increasing infrastructure development, while North America and Europe continue to lead in innovation and adoption of eco-friendly products.</p></p>
