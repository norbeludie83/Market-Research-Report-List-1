<p><p>The workplace drug testing service market encompasses several testing types, each with unique characteristics. Urine drug testing is the most prevalent, offering a cost-effective and reliable method for detecting a range of substances over a short timeframe. Oral fluid drug testing provides a less invasive alternative, allowing for immediate results but typically detecting substances used more recently.
