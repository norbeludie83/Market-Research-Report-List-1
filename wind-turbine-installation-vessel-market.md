<p><ul><li>Self-propelled Jack-up Vessel</li><li>Normal Jack-up Vessel</li><li>Heavy Lift Vessel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wind Turbine Installation Vessel market primarily consists of three types: Self-propelled Jack-up Vessels, Normal Jack-up Vessels, and Heavy Lift Vessels. Self-propelled Jack-up Vessels can maneuver to installation sites without assistance, enhancing operational efficiency. Normal Jack-up Vessels are towed to locations and lifted using their jacking systems, suitable for shallow waters. Heavy Lift Vessels are equipped for hoisting heavy components, ideal for complex installations. Each type caters to specific offshore wind farm requirements, optimizing turbine deployment and installation.</p></p>
