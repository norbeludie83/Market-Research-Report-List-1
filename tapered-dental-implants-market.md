<p><strong>The Tapered Dental Implants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Root-form Dental Implants</li><li>Plate-form Dental Implants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tapered dental implants market includes two primary types: root-form and plate-form dental implants. </p><p>Root-form implants resemble natural tooth roots, tapering at the end for better stability in the bone. They are commonly used in situations where there is sufficient bone volume. </p><p>Plate-form implants are flat and often used in cases with minimal bone width. Their design allows for optimal bone coverage when space is limited, making them ideal for patients with specific anatomical challenges.</p></p>
