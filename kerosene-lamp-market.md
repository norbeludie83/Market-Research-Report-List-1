<p><ul><li>Wick Lamp</li><li>Pressure Lamp</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The kerosene lamp market can be divided into two primary types: wick lamps and pressure lamps. Wick lamps operate by soaking a wick in kerosene, allowing fuel to be drawn up for combustion, which provides a steady flame. Conversely, pressure lamps utilize a pressurized fuel system that enhances brightness and efficiency, often employing a mantle for increased light output. Both types serve as reliable lighting solutions, particularly in areas lacking electricity, each catering to specific user needs and preferences.</p></p>
