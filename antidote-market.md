<p><strong>The Antidote Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chemical Antidotes</li><li>Physical Antidotes</li><li>Pharmacological Antidotes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The antidote market encompasses various types designed to counteract poisoning or harmful effects of substances. Chemical antidotes neutralize toxins at a molecular level, while physical antidotes act by removing or binding toxic substances from the body, enhancing elimination. Pharmacological antidotes work by inhibiting the effects of specific toxins through physiological mechanisms, often mimicking or blocking the action of the harmful agents. Together, these antidotes form a crucial part of medical treatment, improving patient outcomes in toxicological emergencies.</p></p>
