<p><ul><li>Silver-based</li><li>Copper-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrical contact materials market primarily includes silver-based and copper-based categories. Silver-based materials, known for their excellent electrical conductivity and resistance to arc degradation, are widely used in switches and relays. Their high reliability enhances performance in demanding applications. Conversely, copper-based materials offer good conductivity and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for various industrial applications. While silver-based contacts are ideal for high-performance scenarios, copper-based options serve as a budget-friendly alternative for less critical uses in electrical systems.</p></p>
