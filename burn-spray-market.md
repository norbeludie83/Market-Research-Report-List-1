<p><ul><li>Aerosol</li><li>Non-Aerosol</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The burn spray market is divided into two main types: aerosol and non-aerosol. Aerosol burn sprays utilize pressurized containers to deliver a fine mist, providing even coverage and ease of application. They often contain cooling agents and antiseptics. Non-aerosol burn sprays, on the other hand, typically come in pump or squeeze bottles, allowing for more controlled application without propellants. Both types are designed to alleviate pain and promote healing for burns, though their application methods differ significantly.</p></p>
