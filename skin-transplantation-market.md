<p><strong>The Skin Transplantation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Split-Thickness</li><li>Full-Thickness</li><li>Composite Graft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The skin transplantation market encompasses three primary types: split-thickness grafts, full-thickness grafts, and composite grafts. Split-thickness grafts involve the outer layers of skin and are often used for larger wounds, offering quicker healing. Full-thickness grafts include deeper layers, providing better aesthetic results and durability, suited for smaller defects. Composite grafts consist of various tissue types, including skin and cartilage, used in reconstructive surgeries for complex injuries. Each type serves distinct clinical needs, influencing their applications and market demand.</p></p>
