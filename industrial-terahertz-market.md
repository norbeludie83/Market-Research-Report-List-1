<p><ul><li>Passive Terahertz Imaging</li><li>Active Terahertz Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Terahertz market comprises two main types: Passive Terahertz Imaging and Active Terahertz Imaging. Passive Terahertz Imaging utilizes the natural thermal radiation emitted by objects to create images, making it suitable for non-invasive inspection of materials and structures. In contrast, Active Terahertz Imaging employs external terahertz sources to illuminate the target, enhancing detection capabilities for various applications, including quality control and security. Both technologies are essential for advanced material characterization and non-destructive testing in industrial settings.</p></p>
