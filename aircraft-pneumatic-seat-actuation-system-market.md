<p><p>The Aircraft Pneumatic Seat Actuation System market consists of two primary types: linear and rotary actuation systems. Linear actuation systems utilize pneumatic cylinders to move seats along a straight path, offering precise positioning and easy adjustment for passenger comfort. In contrast, rotary actuation systems employ pneumatic mechanisms that enable rotational movement, allowing for functions such as reclining or swiveling. Both types enhance passenger experience by providing customizable seating options and optimizing space within the aircraft cabin.</p></p>
