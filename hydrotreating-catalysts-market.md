<p><p>The hydrotreating catalysts market is divided into two primary types: load type and non-load type. Load type catalysts involve the use of precious metals or active components that are supported on a carrier material, enhancing their effectiveness in hydrogenation processes. Non-load type catalysts, on the other hand, consist of materials that do not rely on external supports, often exhibiting unique properties that can facilitate hydrotreating reactions more efficiently. Both types play critical roles in refining and improving fuel quality.</p></p>
