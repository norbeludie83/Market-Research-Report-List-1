<p><p>Commercial truck fleet insurance encompasses two main market types: compulsory and optional insurance. Compulsory insurance is mandated by law and typically includes liability coverage, protecting against claims arising from accidents involving the fleet. This coverage ensures that all commercial vehicles meet minimum legal standards. Optional insurance allows fleet operators to customize their coverage based on specific needs, providing additional protection for damages, theft, cargo, and other risks. Together, these insurance types provide comprehensive risk management for commercial trucking operations.</p></p>
