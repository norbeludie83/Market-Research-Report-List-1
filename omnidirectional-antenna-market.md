<p><p>The omnidirectional antenna market encompasses various types, including monopole and dipole antennas. Monopole antennas consist of a single conductive element, radiating signal equally in all directions, ideal for ground-level applications. Dipole antennas, composed of two conductive elements, also provide 360-degree coverage but typically have a larger bandwidth and efficiency. Other types may include patch and loop antennas, which can offer specific applications. The versatility and broad application potential of these antennas drive their growing market demand.</p></p>
