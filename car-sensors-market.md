<p><p>The car sensors market encompasses various types critical for engine performance and emissions control. The Throttle Position Sensor (TPS) monitors the position of the throttle, ensuring optimal air intake. The Manifold Absolute Pressure (MAP) Sensor tracks air pressure in the intake manifold, aiding fuel injection calculations. The Mass Airflow (MAF) Sensor measures the volume and density of incoming air. The Oxygen (O2) Sensor detects exhaust oxygen levels to ensure efficient combustion. Other sensors include temperature, pressure, and position sensors, contributing to vehicle functionality.</p></p>
