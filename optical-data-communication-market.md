<p><p>The Optical Data Communication market encompasses various technologies for transmitting data over optical fibers. Wavelength Division Multiplexing (WDM) enables multiple signals on different wavelengths, enhancing bandwidth. Synchronous Optical Networking (SONET) and Synchronous Digital Hierarchy (SDH) facilitate standardized data transfer across large networks. Fiber Channel is specialized for high-speed data transfer in storage area networks. Other technologies include passive optical networks (PON), optical transport networks (OTN), and optical switch systems, catering to diverse communication needs and applications.</p></p>
