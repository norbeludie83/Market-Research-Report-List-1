<p><p>The Fiber Reinforced Polymer (FRP) panels and sheets market includes various types based on the reinforcing fibers used. Carbon Fiber Reinforced Polymer (CFRP) offers high strength and lightweight properties, making it ideal for aerospace and automotive applications. Glass Fiber Reinforced Polymer (GFRP) is cost-effective and widely used in construction and shipping due to its corrosion resistance. Boron Fiber Reinforced Polymer (BFRP) exhibits superior thermal and radiation resistance, suitable for defense and aerospace. Aramid Fiber Reinforced Polymer (AFRP) provides excellent impact resistance, often used in protective applications.</p></p>
