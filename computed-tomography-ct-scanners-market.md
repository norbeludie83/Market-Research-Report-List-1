<p><p>The Computed Tomography (CT) scanner market is categorized based on the number of slices produced during imaging. Low-slice scanners (below 64 slices) are basic models used for general diagnostic purposes. Medium-slice scanners, specifically 64-slice models, offer improved resolution and speed for more detailed imaging, often utilized in cardiac and oncological assessments. High-slice scanners, featuring over 64 slices, provide advanced imaging capabilities, enabling rapid scans and enhanced image quality for complex diagnostics, including 3D reconstructions and volume imaging.</p></p>
