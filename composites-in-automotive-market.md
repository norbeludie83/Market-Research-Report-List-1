<p><ul><li>Polymer Matrix Composites (PMC)</li><li>Metal Matrix Composites (MMC)</li><li>Ceramic Matrix Composites (CMC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive market utilizes three main types of composites: Polymer Matrix Composites (PMC), which are lightweight and corrosion-resistant, ideal for body panels and interiors; Metal Matrix Composites (MMC), which combine metals with ceramics for enhanced strength and thermal performance, suitable for structural applications; and Ceramic Matrix Composites (CMC), known for their high-temperature resistance, often used in components like braking systems. Each type offers unique properties, allowing for improved performance, efficiency, and safety in vehicle design.</p></p>
