<p><p>The reactive diluent market is categorized into three main types: aliphatic, aromatic, and cycloaliphatic. Aliphatic reactive diluents are typically low in toxicity and are used for their excellent solvent properties and drying time benefits. Aromatic reactive diluents are known for their strong solvency and lower volatility, contributing to higher performance in coatings and adhesives. Cycloaliphatic reactive diluents offer unique chemical structures that enhance the durability and stability of formulations, making them popular in high-performance applications.</p></p>
