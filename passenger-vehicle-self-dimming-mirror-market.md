<p><p>The passenger vehicle self-dimming mirror market is divided into two main types: outer self-dimming mirrors and inside self-dimming mirrors. Outer self-dimming mirrors are mounted on the outside of vehicles and automatically adjust their tint to reduce glare from headlights of vehicles behind, enhancing nighttime driving safety. Inside self-dimming mirrors, typically placed on the windshield, serve a similar purpose, adjusting visibility for the driver while minimizing distractions. Both types aim to improve driving comfort and safety by managing reflective glare.</p></p>
