<p><p>The IC substrate packaging market consists of three primary types: metal, ceramics, and glass. Metal substrates, often used for their excellent thermal conductivity, are essential in high-power applications. Ceramics provide superior thermal and mechanical stability, making them ideal for demanding environments. Glass substrates are gaining popularity due to their low dielectric constant and excellent insulation properties, allowing for miniaturization and high-frequency performance. Each material type serves specific applications, catering to varied industry requirements for integrated circuits.</p></p>
