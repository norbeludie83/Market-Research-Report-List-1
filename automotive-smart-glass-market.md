<p><p>The Automotive Smart Glass market includes technologies like Electrochromics, which use electric currents to change glass opacity for light control and privacy. Polymer-Dispersed Liquid Crystal (PDLC) glass instantly switches between transparent and opaque states by aligning liquid crystals with electricity. Suspended Particle Device (SPD) glass adjusts light transmission by aligning particles in response to voltage, offering dynamic shading. Other technologies include thermochromic and photochromic solutions, which respond to temperature or light. These innovations enhance comfort, energy efficiency, and aesthetics in vehicles.</p></p>
