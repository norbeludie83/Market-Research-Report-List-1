<p><p>Reflective materials for wearing apparel primarily include glass beads and microprismatic types. Glass beads are small, spherical elements embedded in fabrics, reflecting light effectively and enhancing visibility in low-light conditions. Microprismatic materials utilize tiny prism structures that redirect light, providing superior reflectivity compared to standard reflective fabrics. Both types serve critical functions in safety wear, promoting visibility for workers, cyclists, and pedestrians. They are increasingly incorporated into fashion and sportswear, blending safety with style.</p></p>
