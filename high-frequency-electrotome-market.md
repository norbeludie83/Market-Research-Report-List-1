<p><strong>The High-Frequency Electrotome Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monopole Electrosurgical Generator</li><li>Bipolar Electrosurgical Generator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High-Frequency Electrotome market comprises two primary types: monopole and bipolar electrosurgical generators. Monopole electrosurgical generators utilize a single active electrode, delivering high-frequency current through the tissue to cut and coagulate. In contrast, bipolar electrosurgical generators use two electrodes, allowing for better control and precision in tissue dissection with reduced thermal damage. Each generator type serves different clinical needs, influencing their applications in surgeries, tissue management, and overall surgical outcomes.</p></p>
