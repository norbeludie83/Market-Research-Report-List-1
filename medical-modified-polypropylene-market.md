<p><strong>The Medical Modified Polypropylene Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Physical Modification</li><li>Chemical Modification</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical Modified Polypropylene Market is categorized into two primary types: physical modification and chemical modification. Physical modification involves altering the polypropylene structure through processes like blending or compounding, enhancing properties such as flexibility, tensile strength, and clarity for medical applications. Chemical modification, on the other hand, entails changing the polymer's chemical structure, often through processes like grafting or cross-linking, to improve biocompatibility, barrier properties, and thermal stability, making it suitable for diverse medical uses such as implants and packaging.</p></p>
